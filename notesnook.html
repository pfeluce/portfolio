Notesnook

Design concept

I find open source projects very cool but they usually are lack of design. So, when I first noticed that Notesnook is a service that is ‘like Evernote but better’, and it looked very Evernote-like, I decided to create a design concept that can be freely used by Notesnook. The main idea was to create unique identity for this service that would be not only free to use but very modern and elegant
